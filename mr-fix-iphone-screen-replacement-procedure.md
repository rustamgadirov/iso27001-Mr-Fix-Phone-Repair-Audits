## **Purpose**  
To ensure safe and correct iPhone screen replacement and reduce the risk of damaging customer devices during repair.

---

## **Scope**  
This procedure applies to all technicians responsible for repairing customer iPhones at Mr Fix Phone Repair.

---

## **Procedure Steps**

1. Ensure the iPhone is completely powered off.  
2. Remove the bottom pentalobe screws using a star-head screwdriver.  
3. Place the device on the heat pad and gently warm the edges to loosen the adhesive.  
4. Use a plastic opening tool to lift the screen from the bottom edge.  
5. Open the device carefully like a book, ensuring internal cables are not stretched.  
6. Remove screws securing the internal shields using a Phillips screwdriver.  
7. Attach an anti-static wrist strap to prevent electrostatic discharge.  
8. Disconnect the battery using a plastic spudger to eliminate power flow.  
9. Gently disconnect all display cables from the motherboard.  
10. Transfer required components (ear speaker, sensors, camera, home button, etc.) from the old screen to the new one.  
11. Reconnect the new display cables to the motherboard.  
12. Reconnect the battery and reinstall the internal metal shields.  
13. Close the device, apply firm pressure around the screen frame, and reinstall the bottom screws.  
14. Power on the device and test:  
   - Touch functionality  
   - Display brightness and color  
   - Face ID or Touch ID  
   - Front/ear speaker  
   - Proximity sensor  
15. Verify all components function correctly and complete the repair.

---

## **Responsibilities**  
- Technicians must follow this procedure for every iPhone screen replacement.  
- Senior technicians must supervise new staff until proper repair techniques are demonstrated.

---

## **Recordkeeping**  
Technicians must close the repair ticket in the system and document the successful completion of the repair.
