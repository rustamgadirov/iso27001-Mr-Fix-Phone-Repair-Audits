# ISO 27001 Control Testing Checklist – A.8, A.10, A.12

This checklist documents internal audit control testing for selected ISO 27001 Annex A controls related to asset management, cryptographic controls, and operations security.

---

## Control: A.8 – Asset Management

**Question:**  
Does the organization maintain a documented inventory of all information assets, including computers, repair devices, software systems, and customer data?

**Evidence:**  
Management was unable to provide a documented asset inventory for computers, repair equipment, or software systems used in the shop. Employees confirmed that devices are tracked informally without written documentation or centralized tracking.

**Status:**  
Gap – Medium Risk

---

## Control: A.10 – Cryptographic Controls

**Question:**  
Are sensitive information and authentication data protected using appropriate encryption methods?

**Evidence:**  
The shop stores system passwords in a plain text file on the office computer without encryption or password management software. No encryption controls or password management tools were observed during the review.

**Status:**  
Gap – High Risk

---

## Control: A.12 – Operations Security

**Question:**  
Are documented procedures available for routine IT operations such as device repairs, system maintenance, and data handling?

**Evidence:**  
Employees confirmed that repair procedures are based on personal experience and verbal guidance. No documented Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) were provided for review.

**Status:**  
Gap – Medium Risk
