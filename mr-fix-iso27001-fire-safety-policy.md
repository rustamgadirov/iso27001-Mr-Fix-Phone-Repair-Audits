Fire Safety Policy (A.7)

Purpose:
To ensure a safe working environment and reduce the risk of fire hazards or accidents within the shop.

Scope:
This policy applies to all employees working in the shop.

Policy Requirements:

The shop must maintain enough functioning smoke detectors in appropriate areas.
The shop must maintain fully charged and accessible fire extinguishers.
The shop must maintain at least two easily accessible exit routes.
Smoke detectors and alarm systems must be tested monthly to confirm proper functionality.

Responsibilities:

Management is responsible for training all staff on fire safety procedures and proper use of fire extinguishers.
Employees must follow evacuation instructions and report any damaged fire safety equipment immediately.

Enforcement:
Failure to follow this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to termination if behavior creates safety risks.
